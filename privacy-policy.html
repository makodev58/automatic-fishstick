Privacy Policy

E8 AURA - Protecting Your Privacy

Last Updated: December 2024
Effective Date: December 2024

1. Introduction

makodev58, a registered entity in Singapore ("we," "our," or "us"), operates the E8 AURA platform ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational technology platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Educational level and preferences
  • Quiz responses and learning progress
  • Account settings and preferences

2.2 Technical Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage data and interaction patterns
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.3 Educational Content

  • Quiz attempts and scores
  • Custom content uploaded by users
  • Handwriting samples (processed via OCR)
  • Learning analytics and progress data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the E8 AURA service
  • Personalizing your learning experience
  • Generating AI-powered quiz content
  • Tracking learning progress and analytics
  • Improving our platform and services
  • Communicating with you about the service
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing misuse

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., cloud hosting, AI processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure cloud infrastructure and backup systems

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Children's Privacy

E8 AURA is designed for family learning and may be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information by contacting us.

8. International Data Transfers

As a Singapore-based company, your data may be processed in Singapore or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

9. Retention Period

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Account data is typically retained until account deletion, while learning data may be retained longer for analytics purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.
